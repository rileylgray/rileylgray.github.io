My Work

Below are some project I have worked on. Each one was either for school or to teach myself something new. I enjoy being able to create almost anything from nothing and coding gives me that opportunity.

Dodge Square

A simple game made in java, with the goal of dodging and staying alive as long as possible. I keep adding to the game so stay with me. The game includes major and minor enemies. Some chase you, some shoot at you, some just bounce around hoping to hit you. You can upgrade yourself but each upgrade reduces your score so play smart and score big.

About Me

My Name is Riley Gray. I am a Sheridan College student in the Software Development and Network Engineering program.

After graduating high school, I headed straight to Humber College and enrolled in the Electrical Techniques program. This was a one-year program in which I graduated with honors. After this program and a few months of work I decide to head to Sheridan College to pursue software development. I am currently in my 2nd year of my 3-year program and have already learned languages like java, HTML, CSS, javascript, and MySQL. I am learning even more through Sheridan and my own pursuits.

My work experience in the IT field is just starting with me landing my first co-op job at the County of Wellington. But before that I spent 4 years working at Dixie Orchards providing assistance to customers. 2 of these years was spent as a manager training and guiding new employees. I also spent a period in my life working as an electrician apprentice for Sommerville electric. Although a short time I learnt many transferable skills like teamwork and problem solving that will aid me in my future.
